Our Design Process

1

Consultation

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision, style preferences, and budget. We'll discuss color palettes, flower varieties, and overall aesthetic to ensure we capture your dream perfectly.

2

Custom Proposal

Based on our consultation, we'll create a detailed proposal including design concepts, flower selections, and comprehensive pricing to match your vision and budget.

3

Design Refinement

We'll work closely with you to refine the designs, making adjustments until every detail is exactly as you envision it. We may create sample arrangements for your approval.

4

Execution

On your wedding day, our team will handle all setup and installation, ensuring every arrangement is perfectly placed and fresh. We coordinate with your venue and other vendors for seamless execution.
