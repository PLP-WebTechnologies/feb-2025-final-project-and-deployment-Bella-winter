Frequently Asked Questions

When should I book my wedding flowers?

We recommend booking your wedding flowers 6-12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season dates (May-October). This ensures availability and gives us ample time for planning and design.

Do you have a minimum budget requirement?

Our full-service wedding florals typically start at Ksh25,500, but we offer smaller à la carte options for intimate weddings or elopements. During your consultation, we'll work with you to maximize your budget while achieving your desired look.

What is your payment schedule?

We require a 25% non-refundable deposit to secure your date, with 50% due three months before your wedding and the final 25% due two weeks prior to your event.

Can I see a sample of my centerpieces before the wedding?

Yes, we offer mock-up appointments for our clients. These can be scheduled approximately 2-3 months before your wedding and include a sample centerpiece and bridal bouquet. Mock-ups are available for an additional fee which is then credited toward your final balance.

Do you provide rentals like vases and arches?

Yes, we offer a variety of vases, pedestals, arches, and other decor items for rent. All rental items are listed in your proposal with their respective prices.
